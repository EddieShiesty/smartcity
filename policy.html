Returns & Refunds

Our promise: If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect product, we will replace it or give you a full refund. No long arguments, no stress.

What can be returned

  • Items received damaged or broken on delivery
  • Wrong item sent (different from what you ordered)
  • Items that stop working within 7 days of delivery (manufacturing defect)
  • Sealed items returned unopened within 7 days of delivery

What cannot be returned

  • Items damaged by the customer after delivery (drops, water damage, etc.)
  • Items returned more than 7 days after delivery
  • Items without original packaging that show signs of use
  • Earphones or earbuds that have been used (for hygiene reasons)

How to start a return

1

Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order via WhatsApp on +254 700 000 000 or email us at smartcity@email.com.

2

Send us a photo of the item showing the issue. This helps us process your return faster.

3

We will confirm your return within 24 hours and arrange collection or advise on drop-off.

4

Refund or replacement will be processed within 3–5 business days after we receive the returned item.

Order processing

  • Orders placed before 2PM are dispatched same day (Mon–Sat)
  • Orders placed after 2PM or on Sunday are dispatched next business day
  • You will receive a WhatsApp message with your order ID and tracking details
  • For urgent deliveries, contact us directly on WhatsApp to arrange express delivery
⚠️ Delivery delays: During public holidays, heavy traffic, or bad weather, delivery may take longer than usual. We will always update you via WhatsApp if there is a delay.

Payments

We accept the following payment methods for all online and in-person orders:

  • M-Pesa (Paybill or Till number — details shared at checkout)
  • Visa and Mastercard debit/credit cards
  • Cash on delivery (Nairobi orders only, arranged via WhatsApp)

Payment security

All card payments are processed securely. We do not store your full card number or CVV on our servers. M-Pesa payments are processed directly through Safaricom's secure network.

Deposits & confirmations

  • For orders over KES 10,000, a 50% deposit may be required before dispatch
  • BnB bookings require a 50% deposit to hold your room
  • All payments are confirmed via M-Pesa SMS or email receipt
⚠️ Important: Never send money to a personal number without first confirming on our official WhatsApp or website. SmartCity will never ask you to pay through an informal channel.

Privacy Policy

SmartCity is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe.

What information we collect

  • Your name, phone number, and email when you place an order or make a booking
  • Your delivery address for shipping purposes
  • Your payment details (processed securely — we don't store card numbers)
  • Device and browser type when you visit our website (anonymous)

How we use your information

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To send you order updates via WhatsApp or SMS
  • To contact you if there is an issue with your order
  • To improve our website and services (anonymised data only)

What we never do

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your contact details without your permission
  • Send spam or unsolicited marketing messages
  • Store your full payment card details
📋

Your Data Rights

You have full rights over your personal data. At any time, you can:

  • Request to see what information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct any incorrect information
  • Ask us to delete your data from our records
  • Opt out of marketing messages at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us on WhatsApp at +254 700 000 000 or email smartcity@email.com and we will respond within 5 business days.

Cookies

Our website uses localStorage (in your browser) to save your cart items between visits. This data never leaves your device and is not sent to any server. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking.

Cancellations & refunds

  • Cancellations made more than 24 hours before check-in: full deposit refund
  • Cancellations made less than 24 hours before check-in: 50% of deposit refunded
  • No-shows (no notice given): deposit is forfeited
  • Refunds are processed via M-Pesa within 3 business days

House rules

  • Guests must present a valid ID at check-in
  • No smoking inside any room — designated smoking area outside
  • No parties or loud music after 10:00 PM
  • All visitors must be registered at reception
  • Pets are not allowed on the premises
  • Any damage to property will be charged to the guest

Restaurant & Food Policy

Orders & reservations

  • Walk-ins are welcome during opening hours — no reservation needed for groups under 6
  • Groups of 6 or more should call or WhatsApp ahead to reserve a table
  • Takeaway orders can be placed via WhatsApp and collected within 20–30 minutes
  • Local delivery available within a 3km radius for orders over KES 500

Food allergens

Please inform our staff of any food allergies or dietary requirements before ordering. While we take care to avoid cross-contamination, our kitchen handles nuts, gluten, dairy, and eggs.

Cancellations (pre-orders)

  • Pre-orders cancelled more than 1 hour before pickup: full refund
  • Pre-orders cancelled less than 1 hour before: 50% refund
  • Food that has already been prepared cannot be refunded
Weekend Brunch Buffet: Buffet tickets are non-refundable but can be transferred to another person. Please inform us in advance if you need to transfer your booking.
